The Four Phases

Quarterly Evolution

Each quarter builds on the last — from laying foundations to establishing your legacy. This is the arc of your transformation.

Q1

Foundation

February 18 – March 31

  • Spanish (2×/week) + French (1×/week) + Saturday deep dives
  • Start first online course — Leadership or Real Estate Finance
  • Establish the 5 AM gym habit — make it non-negotiable
  • Research 2–3 local organizations aligned with your values
Milestone → Hold a basic 10-minute Spanish conversation

Q2

Expansion

April 1 – June 30

  • Add Portuguese on Fridays — leverage Spanish similarities
  • Complete first course, start second
  • Routine is autopilot — push for new PRs
  • First volunteer commitment — 1×/month
Milestone → Basic French conversation, Portuguese fundamentals started

Q3

Deepening

July 1 – September 30

  • Maintain Spanish & French, grow Portuguese
  • Apply coursework to Avenue Homes operations
  • Identity shift complete — you are a person who works out
  • Increase volunteering to 2×/month, explore board seats
Milestone → Comfortable in 3 languages, recognized in your community

Q4

Legacy

October 1 – December 31

  • Introduce Arabic — connecting to Jordanian inspiration
  • Capstone course or certification
  • Year-end transformation photos — celebrate the discipline
  • Scope Avenue Homes charitable initiative
Milestone → 4 languages in progress, philanthropic identity established
